The Primals - Bare Bones



Bio

The band covers music from Final Fantasy XIV in a metal style, the majority of which was composed by Soken.
The band is named after the primals, the summoned monsters of Final Fantasy XIV.


Members

  • Masayoshi Soken
  • Eikichi Iwai
  • GUNN
  • Michael-Christopher Koji Fox
  • Tetsuya Tachibana
